Smart Water Quality Testing

Using advanced sensor technology, AquaGuard instantly analyzes your water to detect contaminants, including bacteria, heavy metals, chlorine, pesticides, and more.
With just a quick dip, you get real-time data on whether your water is safe for drinking, cooking, or general use.

Water Safety Insights & Education

We believe that knowledge is a key step to safeguarding your health and well-being. Our goal is to empower you with the insights you need to understand water quality and its impact on your life. With our educational resources, you get more than just a device — you gain the knowledge to make smarter, safer choices when it comes to your water.

Calibration & Maintenance

Accuracy is everything.
We offer optional calibration services and long-term maintenance plans to keep your device sharp and reliable, whether you're using it at home, school, workplace, etc.

Device Sales and Support

Purchase your AquaGuard unit directly from our website or authorized partners.

Alongside our sales services, we offer full support to ensure customers get the most out of their devices — including setup guidance, troubleshooting assistance, and maintenance advice. Our goal is to make sure every device we provide continues to deliver trusted results, backed by a team committed to excellent service and ongoing care.

Job Opportunities

At AquaGuard, we value growth and innovation, not just in the products we create but also in the team we build. We actively seek to bring new talent into our company.

Our team is essential to achieving our mission of clean and safe water for all, and we continue to invest in the future by nurturing the next generation of leaders in the field.

OpenSource Network

We believe that collaboration drives progress.
Our open-source network provides access to testing protocols, calibration standards, software tools, and research findings, allowing individuals and organizations to adopt, adapt, and build upon proven water quality solutions.
