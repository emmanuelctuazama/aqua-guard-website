About Us

AquaGuard

AquaGuard is an open-source, solar-power, modular sensing device that can be easily develop to test and treat water contamination and provide real time data of automatically.

At AquaGuard we believe that clean, safe water is a right, and we are committed to making it a reality for everyone. Our team uses advanced technology to test for harmful substances, bacteria, and other risks that can affect the quality of your water. Whether it's for your home, community, or business, we are here to give you peace of mind, knowing that the water you rely on is safe for drinking and everyday use.
Provide remediation of said contaminants, it is designed to low-cost easy to build, making it accessible to communities around the world.
